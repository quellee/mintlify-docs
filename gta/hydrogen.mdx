## Redeeming the Key


<Steps>
    <Step title="First Step">
        After purchasing and receiving the key, go to the [Hydrogen Website](https://hydrogen.ac/auth/register) and create an account.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Second Step">
        Now, login to your created account and press Redeem Key, paste the license key into the field License Key to redeem it and bind it to your account.
    </Step>
</Steps>

## Downloading

<Note>
    Download Blocked for "Malicious Software"? Go to your Browser Histroy (CNTRL + J) and allow the download there.
</Note>

    After successfully redeeming your license, deactivate your Antivirus, press the Button Download to download the Loader.

## Injecting 

<Note>
    Make sure to disable your antivirus before injecting the cheat.
</Note>

<Steps>
    <Step title="First Step">
        Start your Game and fully connect to a server.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Second Step">
        Open up the Loader (.exe) as Administrator and sign in with your Data.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Third Step">
        Select Cheat to inject from the left side (Alt:V or Rage:MP) and then on the Blue Inject Button.
    </Step>
        <Step title="Fourth Step">
        The Loader will inject and then close automatically.
    </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
    After injecting the cheat, you can use the cheat menu by pressing the `INSERT` key. 
</Tip>

## Common Issues

<AccordionGroup>
    <Accordion title="Bluescreen while RAGE:MP injection">
        UNINSTALL (not only deactivate) all third party AntiVirus (e.g. Avast, Bitdefender).
    </Accordion>
    <Accordion title="Hydrogen is not Streamproof">
        Launch OBS: Add Game recording -> select GTA5 -> wait until the Game shows up in the OBS preview then inject.
    </Accordion>
    <Accordion title="alt:V is crashing on inject">
        Don't inject on alt:V Main Menu, wait until you fully joined onto a Server and make sure you run alt:V as admin.
    </Accordion>
    <Accordion title="RAGE:MP injection is just infinitely loading">
        Don't inject on Rage:MP Main Menu, wait until you fully joined onto a Server and make sure you run Rage:MP as admin.
    </Accordion>
    <Accordion title="I can't see the menu after injection.">
        If you have Steam GTA make sure that Steam Overlay can be opened Ingame, if you don't have Steam GTA do the same thing as for the Streamproof fix.
    </Accordion> 
</AccordionGroup>
