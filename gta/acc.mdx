## Delivery Info

After your account has been delivered, you will get your Account Data in the following format:

<Info>
    `E-Mail:Password`

    Example: `abcdefg@rambler.ru:Hello123!`
</Info>

In this Case your Account Data would be the same for all services, so:

<Info>
Epic Games/Rockstar Games/Mail Platform Login:

Mail: `abcdefg@rambler.ru`

Password: `Hello123!`
</Info>

<Warning>
    In some Cases, your Account will be delivered in this Form:

    `E-Mail:Password (Epic Password)`

    Example: `abcdefg@rambler.ru:Hello123! (Epic Hello14455!)`

    In this Case your Account Data would be the same for Rockstar and Mail Platform. Your Epic Games Password would be Hello14455! in this Case.
</Warning>
