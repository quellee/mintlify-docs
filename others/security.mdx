---
title: 'Others - Uninstall Security  Update'
description: 'Learn how to uninstall the Security  Update.'
icon: 'stars'
---

<Steps>
    <Step title="First Step">
        Go to `Settings > Windows Update. Choose Pause Updates` for `1 Week`.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Second Step">
        Go to `Settings > Windows Update > Update history > Uninstall` updates.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Third Step">
        Uninstall the Update called `Security Updates for Microsoft Windows`.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Fourth Step">
        Restart your PC.
    </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
    If you can't find the Update, you may have reinstall your Windows.
</Warning>

<Info>
    After restarting your PC, the newest Security Updates are gone.
</Info>
