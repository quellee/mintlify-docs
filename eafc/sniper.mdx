## Set Up

<Steps>
  <Step title="First Step">
    Go on the link we provided you and register an account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Second Step">
    Download the Chrome Extension [FUT Sniping Bot](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/fut-sniping-bot/bflpmpggmkamcjjdjajcafkpcmjmgcfg) from here.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Third Step">
    After you have downloaded the extension, click on the extension icon in the top right corner of your browser. Enter the username and password you registered with on the website and then click on the `Login` button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fourth Step">
    Finally, click on `Go to FUT Web App` and you should see the Sniping Bot interface as soon as you load in.
  </Step>
</Steps>
